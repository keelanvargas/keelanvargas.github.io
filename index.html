About Me

Hi, I’m Keelan. I’m a geospatial professional and cartographer with a passion for designing clear, functional GIS systems, maps, and web applications. I bring a broad technical skill set and experience across multiple industries, giving me a strong foundation in spatial analysis, data management, and cartographic design. I enjoy building tools and visualizations that are both intuitive and informative. Thanks for stopping by—feel free to explore my work below.
